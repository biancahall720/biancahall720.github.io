About Us

Meet the F.A.M.!

We are a group of 5 girls who are interested in coding and want to make a difference in the world. Since we all have taken the opportunity to participate in Girls Who Code we made a game in order to combat Islamaphobia.We wanted to combine our loves of fashion, coding, and awareness of social issues to come up a project that could help others. It all started when we all played dress up game as a kids and noticed the lack of diversity in the games. We wanted to see more characters with clothing from different cultures, as well as representative of different religions. For example in Islam, clothing is more modest, and we wanted clothing choices in the game to reflect that. By introducing young girls to people who look different than what society depicts them as, young girls get a wider perspective on the world.

Mission

To tackle the lack of diversity in games and to spread awareness about Islamaphobia.
